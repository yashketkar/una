About Us

The Indiana University United Nations Association Chapter was created in January of 2014. Although we are a new organization, we have many plans for the near future! Our new club on the Indiana University Campus will host events, dinners, listen to think-tanks and attend congressional hearings.

We are excited to have you join us on this excited journey of becoming global advocates!
